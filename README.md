## 🛑 THE PROBLEM

You open YouTube to watch a classic 2012 Minecraft Let's Play.
Next thing you know, it's 3 AM, you are 400 deep into AI-generated Shorts, ads are yelling at you to buy crypto, and your recommendations are filled with *"I SURVIVED 50 HOURS IN A BEANBAG"* videos.

**Modern YouTube is loud. The Golden Era was immaculate.**

## 🦸‍♂️ THE SOLUTION

**YouTube Nostalgia Filter** is a lightweight, purely client-side Chrome Extension that violently ejects modern garbage from your feed and only shows you the *good stuff*. You pick the year threshold. We handle the rest.

Want to browse YouTube like it's 2014? Set the slider to **10 Years Ago**. We will legally blind you to anything uploaded after that.

✨ *Experience the classic internet without the clutter!* ✨

---

## 🔥 FEATURES

- 🚀 **Time-Travel Tech:** Ruthlessly hides any video uploaded more recently than your chosen threshold. (e.g., only show videos older than 8 years).
- 🧽 **Aggressive Junk Annihilator:** Automatically detects and vaporizes:
  - 🗑️ YouTube Shorts (begone, fast-paced poison!)
  - 🛑 Ads & Promoted Streams
  - 📦 Worthless recommendation shelves
- 💎 **Ultra-Premium UI:** A breathtaking, glassmorphic popup that looks like it was designed by a top-tier Silicon Valley firm in 2030, just to help you browse 2010.
- 🧠 **Smart Memory:** Your nostalgia configuration is saved locally. Close the browser? Restart your PC? The filter remembers.
- ⚡ **Real-Time Engine:** Powered by a hyper-optimized `MutationObserver`. As you scroll, modern videos are purged before your brain even processes they exist.

## 🛠️ INSTALLATION (Be a Hacker)

This extension is so powerful, it's not even on the web store yet. You have to install it like a true developer.

1. **Clone or Download** this repository.

   ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/nareshAiNexus/nostalgic-youtube.git
   ```

2. Open your browser and punch in the developer portal:
   - Chrome: `chrome://extensions/`
   - Edge: `edge://extensions/`
3. Toggle that **Developer mode** switch in the top right corner. You're basically Neo now. 💊
4. Click **Load unpacked**.
5. Select the `youtube-nostalgia` folder.
6. **BOOM.** Pin it to your toolbar and prepare for a blast from the past.

---

## 🎮 HOW TO USE

1. Boot up [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com).
2. Click the insanely clean extension icon in your toolbar.
3. Rip that **Slider** to your desired "Nostalgia Year" (e.g. 5, 8, 15+ years ago).
4. Smash the **Apply Time Filter** button.
5. Watch in awe as the algorithm bends to your will and the modern world fades away!

Because we are professionals here.
